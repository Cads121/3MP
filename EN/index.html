About Us

3MP is a company created by 2 friends, with a lot of experience in the area of advertising and decoration, who decided to unite their talents in 2017, with the "IMPOSSIBLE UNTIL DONE!" philosophy!

With Great dedication, innovation, creativity and commitment, we already have more than 500 projects, and more than 15000m² of stands assembled in Europe and Africa.

We are composed by a team of demanding professionals, with advertising in the DNA.We create, develop and execute all the challenges we are offered. We are resilient!We reside at Linhó - Sintra - Lisbon - Portugal, but borderless to spread our magic.
